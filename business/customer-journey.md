# Customer Journey Map for Mesh-Sentry

## Aware
- **Trigger Event**: DevOps teams experience challenges with managing and visualizing service meshes, leading to inefficiencies.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about service mesh management tools; existing solutions are complex or not tailored to specific needs.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration, confusion, and a sense of urgency to find a solution.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Educational content (webinars, blogs) that simplifies service mesh concepts; case studies showcasing successful implementations.
- **Metric**: Number of website visits and engagement rates on educational content.

## Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Teams begin researching solutions after recognizing inefficiencies in their current processes.
- **Friction Points**: Overwhelming number of options; difficulty in comparing features and benefits; skepticism about claims made by vendors.
- **User Emotions**: Hopeful but cautious; feeling overwhelmed by choices.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Interactive comparison tools; clear, concise product documentation; customer testimonials and success stories.
- **Metric**: Number of demo requests and product page views.

## Try
- **Trigger Event**: Teams decide to test Mesh-Sentry after initial research and comparisons.
- **Friction Points**: Installation and setup complexity; lack of clear onboarding resources; potential integration issues with existing tools.
- **User Emotions**: Excitement mixed with anxiety about the implementation process.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Streamlined onboarding process; step-by-step guides and video tutorials; responsive customer support during trial.
- **Metric**: Trial activation rates and time taken to complete setup.

## Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Teams successfully integrate Mesh-Sentry into their workflow and begin using it regularly.
- **Friction Points**: Initial learning curve; potential resistance from team members accustomed to existing tools.
- **User Emotions**: Satisfaction from solving pain points but also frustration with adapting to new workflows.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Ongoing training sessions; community forums for peer support; regular check-ins from customer success teams.
- **Metric**: User retention rates and frequency of tool usage.

## Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Teams recognize additional features or capabilities of Mesh-Sentry that could further optimize their processes.
- **Friction Points**: Uncertainty about how to leverage advanced features; potential budget constraints for scaling.
- **User Emotions**: Enthusiasm for potential improvements but also concern about costs and resource allocation.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Personalized recommendations for feature usage; flexible pricing models for scaling; success stories of other teams expanding their use.
- **Metric**: Upsell conversion rates and feature adoption rates.
